Our Partners

Our partners have been an integral part of the Pads.Now program, and your support goes beyond providing hygiene products to removing barriers for countless women and girls so they can study, work and live. Your cooperation will promote social attention to menstrual health, break down long-standing prejudices, so that every woman can have the care and support they deserve. Only by working together can we truly create a more equitable and inclusive world where periods are no longer a barrier to women’s advancement, but a natural part that is respected and valued.
